BIO

Kyle founded Backup Parachute Software over 12 years ago, concentrating on developing and managing multiple in-house SaaS platforms. With 25 years of technical experience, he has delivered hundreds of successful projects for a diverse range of clients, including Fortune 100 and 500 companies, large hedge funds, small and medium sized businesses, government agencies, and military projects requiring clearance.

As a fractional CTO, he provides strategic guidance to help companies navigate complex technical challenges and drive growth. His background as a chief engineer ensures that his solutions are scalable, maintainable, and built to last.

As a seasoned SaaS operator with one successful exit, he truly has full stack experience, designing, developing, operating, marketing, and selling software.

Kyle brings a unique perspective on creating solutions that solve real-world problems, and translating customer problems into super-sticky products that customers love. Whether you are looking to streamline operations, hire technical staff, improve customer experience, or drive growth, he has the expertise to help you achieve your goals.

While he has extensive experience assisting large companys, he now prefers to focus on helping smaller businesses with annual revenues ranging from a few hundred thousand to a few million.
